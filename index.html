Our Story

Rooted in Tradition,
Worn with Pride

Pavaswi Panchaloham Jewellery was born from a deep reverence for India's sacred jewellery traditions. We specialise exclusively in authentic Panchaloham — the five-metal alloy considered auspicious in Vedic culture, combining gold, silver, copper, brass and iron.

From bridal sets to daily-wear chains, jhumkas to bangles — every piece we craft is a testament to heritage artisanship and devotion. Trusted by thousands of families in Hyderabad and beyond.

We believe jewellery is not just an adornment — it is a blessing you wear every day.
